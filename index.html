Tillson Galloway for FFM Finance Chair

Hi, I'm Tillson Galloway, a Montag 1 resident, and I'm running for the Finance Chair position on FFM's Hall Council.
What follows is a simple page to argue for my case and to outline my primary goals if elected. I hope you will take the time to give it a read.

Regardless of who you decide to vote for, please come out to vote on Tuesday at 9:00 PM (click to add to calendar).

Experience

I have actively participated in discussions at weekly Hall Council meetings throughout the Summer and Fall semesters. This has provided me with experience in understanding how RHA's legislative process works, how to best present the financial state of the community every week, and most importantly, the ways in which money is spent within the FFM community. During the Fall Semester, I authored a successful amendment to the RHA bylaws. The amendment, passed unanimously by the General Assembly, resolved a multi-week conflict regarding the potential for one floor to obtain a majority in order to dominate the vote and to pass absurd and expensive bills. The amendment created a new requirement for a 2/3 vote to pass bills costing over $75.

Goals

If elected Finance Chair, I'll create an automated, email-based system for reimbursements with three key features:

  • An email delivered when a bill is passed to provide the bill's author a succinct outline of the reimbursement process, with key deadlines attached and emphasized.
  • Emails to proactively reminds bill authors of pending deadlines
  • Confirmation emails after successful completion and processing of each step for 'ease of mind'.

I believe that this system will create a straightforward means of communication between RHA and residents during the reimbursement process.

Additionally, if elected, I will do my best to act in the interest of the FFM community at large both during public Hall Council debates and internal meetings.
